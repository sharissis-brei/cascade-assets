Julianne Argyros Fitness Center

Located between the Hutton Sports Center and Wilson Field

The innovative 4,000 square foot Julianne Argyros Fitness Center creates a welcoming environment for the Chapman community. The Center's mission is to promote wellness and healthy lifestyles by providing a space to host a variety of exercise activities and physical education classes.
